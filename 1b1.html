Traffic Management

Efficient traffic management is critical to reducing congestion and enhancing mobility in urban areas. Using IoT-based sensors and advanced algorithms, traffic systems can optimize vehicle flow, reduce travel times, and improve safety on roads.

Solutions/Plans:

Traffic Flow Optimization with Graph Algorithms

  • Use Dijkstra’s Algorithm: Identify the shortest routes for vehicles, minimizing travel times. By modeling road networks as weighted graphs, this algorithm helps find optimal paths, reducing congestion in real-time.
