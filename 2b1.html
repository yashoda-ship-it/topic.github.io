Emergency Fire Detection

Fire detection systems are crucial for early intervention and minimizing damage. Using IoT-based temperature and smoke sensors, these systems can detect fires in their initial stages. Efficient response plans rely on optimizing fire truck routes and analyzing high-risk areas.

Risk Assessment Using Greedy Algorithms

  • Deploy Sensors: Place sensors in fire-prone zones and use Greedy Algorithms to prioritize high-risk areas for inspection. The algorithm evaluates risk levels based on historical fire data and ensures resources are allocated efficiently to maximize coverage.
  • Optimize Sensor Placement: Utilize Backtracking to ensure all critical areas are covered efficiently. This approach explores all possible configurations of sensor placement to achieve the best coverage with minimal overlap, ensuring no high-risk zones are left unmonitored.

Predictive Monitoring with Divide and Conquer

  • Analyze IoT Data: Use Merge Sort to prioritize regions based on risk intensity. The algorithm sorts regions by their risk scores, allowing emergency teams to focus on the most vulnerable areas first.
  • Locate Anomalies: Use Binary Search to quickly identify and address anomalies. After sorting sensor data, this algorithm pinpoints unusual readings efficiently, enabling prompt intervention before situations escalate.
