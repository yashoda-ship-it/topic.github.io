Smart Street Lighting Portfolio

Business Case: Smart Street Lighting

Subtask: Energy-Efficient Lighting Control

Sustainable Development Goal: SDG Goal 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities)

Target: 11.6

Indicator: 11.6.1

The city faces significant challenges in managing street lighting efficiently, leading to high energy consumption and rising operational costs. The Smart Street Lighting system aims to enhance energy efficiency by incorporating automated controls and sensors that adjust the illumination levels based on real-time conditions, ensuring public safety while minimizing energy usage.
