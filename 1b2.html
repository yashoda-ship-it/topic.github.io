Smart Street Lighting System

Optimizing Urban Street Lighting

Sub-task: Smart Street Lighting

SDG Goal: 7

Target: 7.3

Indicator: 7.3.1

The city has implemented a smart street lighting system to optimize energy consumption and enhance public safety. The system uses sensors and real-time data to adjust lighting levels based on traffic and pedestrian activity. The system integrates real-time data to optimize the streetlights' energy consumption. The key focus areas are:

  • Adjusting light levels based on traffic and pedestrian activity.
  • Minimizing energy consumption during off-peak hours.
  • Improving safety by ensuring adequate lighting during high-traffic times.

Key Components:

  • Input: Traffic and pedestrian sensor data, light control parameters.
  • Output: Optimized streetlight illumination based on real-time data.
  • Control: Centralized hub processing the data and sending commands to lights.

Reflection: This smart system could further integrate with city-wide IoT infrastructure for dynamic updates and energy management, enhancing sustainability.
