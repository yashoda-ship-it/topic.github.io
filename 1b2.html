Business Case: Smart Street Lighting

Subtask: Energy-Efficient Lighting Control

Sustainable Development Goal: SDG Goal 11

Target: 11.6

Indicator: 11.6.1

The city faces significant challenges in managing street lighting efficiently, leading to high energy consumption and rising operational costs. The Smart Street Lighting system aims to enhance energy efficiency by incorporating automated controls and sensors that adjust the illumination levels based on real-time conditions. These include traffic density, pedestrian movement, and ambient light levels, ensuring public safety while minimizing energy usage.

Dynamic Scheduling Algorithm

To minimize energy consumption and ensure public safety, a Dynamic Scheduling Algorithm is employed. The algorithm adjusts the brightness of the streetlights in real-time based on the following parameters:

  • Traffic flow: Higher illumination in busy areas.
  • Pedestrian count: Adjusts lighting in areas with significant pedestrian activity.
  • Ambient light: Reduces lighting in well-lit areas.

Key Components

  • Input: Traffic flow, pedestrian counts, and ambient light levels.
  • Output: Dynamic and energy-efficient lighting schedules for different zones.
  • Algorithm: The system uses a Dynamic Scheduling Algorithm that integrates sensor-based data for real-time adjustments.

System Benefits

  • Energy Efficiency: The system dynamically adjusts street lighting to reduce energy consumption during low-traffic periods.
  • Safety: Ensures proper illumination during high-traffic periods and in critical areas.
  • Sustainability: Integration of renewable energy sources like solar power for a more sustainable operation.

Reflection

This smart street lighting system contributes to SDG Goal 11 by reducing the environmental impact of urban street lighting and enhancing the livability of cities. Further enhancements could include integrating predictive analytics to anticipate energy needs and incorporating smart poles with additional functions such as air quality monitoring, Wi-Fi, and CCTV.
