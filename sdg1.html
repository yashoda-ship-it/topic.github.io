Meanings of SDG 11:

  • SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities - Make cities inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable by improving urban areas and public spaces.
  • Target 11.2: Provide access to safe, affordable, accessible, and sustainable transport systems for all, improving road safety, notably by expanding public transport and providing adequate transport systems for all.
  • Target 11.6: Reduce the adverse per capita environmental impact of cities, particularly by paying attention to air quality and municipal and other waste management.
  • Target 11.7: Provide universal access to safe, inclusive, and accessible green and public spaces, particularly for women and children, the elderly, and persons with disabilities.
