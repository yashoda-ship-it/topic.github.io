Problem Space:

4. Problem Definition

Cities are growing rapidly, leading to problems like traffic congestion, inefficient resource management, pollution, and outdated infrastructure. These issues reduce the quality of life and hinder sustainability. Our project aims to address these problems using smart technologies such as sensors, AI, and automation to enhance transportation, waste management, energy use, and public safety, making cities more efficient, eco-friendly, and livable for everyone.
