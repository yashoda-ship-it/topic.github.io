Smart City Design and Business Cases

Problem Space

The project identifies challenges in city design, reflecting on findings from the white paper. Team members discussed business cases to address these challenges, leading to a well-rounded problem definition and actionable solutions.

City Design and Identification of Business Cases

City design in the white paper emphasizes leveraging IoT to create a connected ecosystem. Business cases were identified by analyzing areas with operational inefficiencies and opportunities for technological intervention.

Business Cases Identified:

  • Shubha C.S.
    • Traffic Management: Addressing congestion with real-time traffic data.
    • Smart Street Lighting: Using sensors for adaptive lighting to save energy.
    • Water & Waste Management: Monitoring systems for efficient resource use.
    • Public Safety: Integrated systems for better security.
    • Energy Management: Smart grids and renewable integration.
  • Yashoda Talawar
    • Emergency Fire Detection: Early fire warnings using IoT.
    • Road Condition Sensors: Real-time alerts to ensure safer roads.
    • Self-Driving Cars: Autonomous systems for efficient transport.
    • Solar Panels: Promoting renewable energy use.
    • Drones: Enhanced urban operations and delivery systems.
  • Preeti Kotabagi
    • Body Cameras: For transparency and improved security.
    • Wearable Detection: Monitoring health in real-time.
    • Fleet Management: Optimizing vehicle operations.
    • Smart Logistics/Freight: Streamlined supply chain.
    • Surveillance Cameras: Ensuring security in public spaces.
